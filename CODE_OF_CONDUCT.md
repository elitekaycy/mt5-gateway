# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

We pledge to make participation harassment-free regardless of age, disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity, experience, education,
nationality, appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

Be respectful, accept constructive feedback, focus on what is best for the
community, and show empathy. Harassment, trolling, personal attacks, sexualized
attention, and publishing private information are unacceptable.

Project maintainers may remove or reject contributions and temporarily or
permanently ban participants for behavior they deem inappropriate. Report
incidents privately to the repository owner through GitHub. This policy follows
Contributor Covenant 2.1.
